Spending Limits

[Daily Spending Limit] will restrict your account to spending the set amount of bitcoin per day. The account password will be required to spend above that amount.

[Require PIN above amount] will require the user to enter their PIN when sending funds above the specified amount. Disabling this setting will have all spends never require a PIN.

Note that a PIN is not required in the first two minutes after logging into your account.

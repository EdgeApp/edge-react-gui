Wallets List

Create new wallets using the [+] button on the WALLETS header. Choose a fiat currency that you would like to use to track the value of transactions in the new wallet. Note that this fiat currency setting cannot be changed once set. You can change the global fiat currency for your account in the Settings tab in the lower right corner.

Rename or delete a wallet by tap and holding a wallet name for 3 seconds

Reorder wallets by dragging the 3-bar icon on the right of each wallet. The top most wallet is your default wallet used to Request and Send transactions when first logged in

Archive wallets by dragging them below the ARCHIVE header. Archived wallets do not show up in the balance in the top toggle bar and cannot be used to Send or Request funds. The Archive may be hidden by tapping the arrow to the right on the ARCHIVE header.
